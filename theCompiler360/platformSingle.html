What Are the Key AML requirements for financial Instruction in Africa?

Here are the key AML requirements under Nigerian regulations:

  • Customer Due Diligence (CDD)

  • Customer Identification and Verification (KYC)

  • Ongoing Monitoring of Transactions

  • Suspicious Transaction Reporting (STR)

What Is the Al Compliance Assistant?

The Al Compliance Assistant is an advanced, domain-trained Al solution built specifically for the financial services industry. It understands complex regulatory frameworks, internal policies, and industry best practices to provide accurate, contextual, and actionable compliance support.

It helps compliance, risk, legal, and audit teams save time, improve accuracy, and stay ahead of evolving regulatory requirements.
